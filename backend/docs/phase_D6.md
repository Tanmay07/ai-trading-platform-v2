# Phase D6: Prediction Feedback Engine & Continuous Learning Platform

## Overview
The Prediction Feedback Engine tracks the complete lifecycle of every trading recommendation generated by the Meta Decision Engine. It measures the theoretical performance of the predictions against actual market data and creates feedback loops for ML retraining.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. State Tracking & Lifecycle
A prediction flows through the following states in the `feedback.db`:
- **Generated**: Decision Engine outputs a signal.
- **Active**: The signal is executed (paper or live), locking in entry price, targets, and stops.
- **Completed**: The holding period ends or an exit condition occurs.
- **Evaluated**: EOD job compares the Active parameters against actual `high_price` and `low_price` to calculate MFE/MAE and hit status.
- **Archived**: Removed from active tables after 30 days.

### 2. Outcome Evaluation
For every evaluated prediction, the system records:
- **Actual Return**: Final exit price vs entry price.
- **Maximum Favorable Excursion (MFE)**: Highest peak profit during the holding period.
- **Maximum Adverse Excursion (MAE)**: Deepest drawdown during the holding period.
- **Hit Target / Hit Stoploss**: Boolean flags based on strict limit touches.

### 3. Explainability Analyzers
- `SuccessAnalyzer`: Tags winning trades with reasons like "Strong Trend" or "Bull Market Tailwind".
- `FailureAnalyzer`: Tags losing trades with reasons like "Market Reversal" or "Bear Market Headwind".

### 4. Continuous Learning (D2 Integration)
- **Confidence Calibration**: Adjusts future ML probabilities based on historical win rates of past predictions in the same confidence bucket.
- **Dataset Builder**: Extracts all evaluated records into a tabular dataset (`feedback_dataset.csv`) to serve as labeled ground-truth for retraining the Feature Store models (Phase D2).

## API Endpoints
- `GET /api/predictions`: Fetch all tracked predictions.
- `POST /api/predictions/evaluate`: Manually trigger evaluation of all "Completed" predictions (Mocks the cron job).
- `POST /api/predictions/build_dataset`: Exports the dataset for D2 retraining.
